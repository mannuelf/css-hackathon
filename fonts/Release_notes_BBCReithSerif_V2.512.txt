==Release notes_BBC Reith Serif==

Compiler of release notes: Dalton Maag
Designer: Dalton Maag
Designer URL: daltonmaag.com

Date of creation: 2019-08-28

Changes since previous release:
- Subsetted numerals.
- Updated plus sign.

Basic information
1 Font name: BBC Reith Serif
2 TrueType Version record: 2.512
3 Regional package: Latin, Cyrillic
4 Supported scripts: Latin, Cyrillic
5 Number of glyphs: 867 Light, Bold and ExtraBold; 901 Regular and Medium; 873 Light Italic, Medium Italic, Bold Italic and ExtraBold Italic; 907 Italic
6 Weights/Styles: Light, Light Italic, Regular, Italic, Medium, Medium Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, ExtraBold, ExtraBold Italic
7 Usage: Text and Display

Technology
1 File formats: The files have been delivered in the TrueType-based OpenType format, for use in all major Operating Systems, and also as TTF for use in apps, and in web font format: EOT; WOFF; WOFF2.
2 Embedding settings: Editing of the document is allowed (editable mode), subsetting is allowed.
3 Hinting information: All glyphs are manually hinted for optimized rasterization in Microsoft ClearType or for other LCD devices. Regular, Italic, Medium, Medium Italic, Bold and Bold Italic have full manual hinting.
4 Default numerals: Tabular. Proportional figures accessible via OpenType features.
